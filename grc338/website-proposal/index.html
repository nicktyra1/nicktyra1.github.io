Introduction

Bighead Hats and Apparel

Bighead is a company that designs, produces, and distributes minimalistic headwear and apparel including t-shirts and sweatshirts.

Target audience

A typical user of this site is anyone interested in simple and fashionable headwear and apparel, looking to learn more about the Bighead brand and browse available products.

The primary tasks and goals of a user of this site are to find information about the company like location, hours, and backstory, as well as effectively and efficiently browsing available products and inventory.

Our Story

Bighead started with a vision of fashionable simplicity. Tired of played out trends in extravagant hat and apparel design, Bighead wanted to build a line of products that speak for themselves without the need for over the top designs and marketing.
